Piano

This is a page about everything piano

The beginning

I started playing piano around the age of 13 and get lessons for it. It took me a few years of off and on playing and practicing before it really stuck. I really started getting into it when I was about 16 years old which was around the time I discovered ragtime piano. Since then my love for piano has grown and is still growing.

More

My favourite genre of piano music for the past year has been ragtime. It is a very jumpy style of playing, quite similar to stride. There are many famous ragtime pianists that you might have heard of, notably; Scott Joplin or Jelly Roll Morton. The most famous pieces you have probably heard like "The Entertainer" or "Maple Leaf Rag", are both Scott Joplin pieces. Although, I mainly play and listen to more contemporary pieces and composers, like Tom Brier, Adam Swanson, Max Keenlyside etc. My favourite piece is probably "Balderdash!" by Tom Brier.
